Privacy policy

officesuitetools.online

While operating Officesuitetools.online, it is important to us to respect the privacy of our users and to collect only the information necessary to manage and optimize our websites and business.

User Information

Officesuitetools.online collects non-identifying information such as browser type, language, date and time of visit, and referring web site. This is common on most websites and is automatically provided by web browsers and servers. Officesuitetools.online collects this information to understand user behavior and to optimize its websites. Officesuitetools.online does not pass on any of this information to third parties.

Officesuitetools.online does not collect personal information such as Internet Protocol (IP) addresses. Certain gps manufacturers listed on the websites of Officesuitetools.online do however offer us access to this information when a referenced user makes a purchase on its website. Officesuitetools.online does not use or sell this information.

Cookies

A cookie is a line of code that stores a website on a user's device and that the user's web browser makes available to the user every time the user returns. Officesuitetools.online uses cookies to learn and analyze user behavior and measure the sales success of our partners.

Users who do not want cookies installed on their devices can set their browser to refuse cookies. However, this may disable certain features, and not all parts of Officesuitetools.online websites may work. properly.

Business-Transfer

Should Officesuitetools.online or their websites get acquired, user information that has been collected may be transferred to third parties.

External links

Officesuitetools.online only links to reputable companies, but has no control over their websites and how they store user information. Any interaction with affiliate websites is therefore subject to their privacy policies and terms of use.

Changes to the privacy policy

Officesuitetools.online may make minor adjustments to the Privacy Policy over time. The continued use of Officesuitetools.online Sites means that a user accepts such changes. The last update was made on September 3, 2023.
